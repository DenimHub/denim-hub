# 🛍️ Denim Hub

Denim Hub is a full-stack inventory and billing management system designed for retail stores to manage products, track sales, and analyze business performance with real-time insights.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🛒 Product Management
- Add, update, and view products
- Manage multiple sizes with different pricing
- Upload and display product images
- Apply discounts on products
- Categorize products for better organization

---

### 📦 Inventory Management
- Track stock quantity per size
- Automatic total stock calculation
- Low stock alerts using minimum stock threshold
- Real-time stock updates after each sale

---

### 💳 Billing System 
- Generate bills with multiple products
- Support for walk-in and registered customers
- Automatic calculation of subtotal, discount, and total amount
- Multiple payment methods: Cash, UPI, Card
- Clean bill preview and print support

---

### 📊 Sales Reports
- View all sales transactions in a structured table
- Filter reports by date range
- Summary metrics:
  - Total Revenue
  - Total Bills
  - Total Items Sold
  - Average Bill Value
- Payment method breakdown (Cash / UPI / Card)
- Export sales data to CSV

---

### 📈 Advanced Analytics Dashboard
- Sales overview (Daily, Weekly, Monthly)
- Top-selling products analysis
- Slow-moving products identification
- Stock valuation tracking
- Profit estimation based on sales data

---

### 🤖 AI-Based Product Insights
- Product lifecycle classification:
  - Best Seller
  - Slow Mover
  - Overstocked
  - Low Stock
  - Out of Stock
- Smart recommendations:
  - Restock alerts
  - Discount suggestions
  - Promotion strategies

---

### 👥 Customer Management
- Store customer details
- Link customers with billing history
- Support for walk-in customers

---

### 🎟️ Coupon System
- Create and manage discount coupons
- Apply coupons during billing process

---

### 📤 Export & Reporting
- Export sales reports as CSV
- Export analytics reports with key metrics
- Useful for business decision-making

---

### 🌙 UI & Experience
- Clean and responsive dashboard UI
- Dark mode support
- Interactive charts using Recharts
- Smooth animations and user-friendly design

---

### ⚙️ Backend
